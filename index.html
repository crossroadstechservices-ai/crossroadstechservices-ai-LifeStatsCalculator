🌍 About Life Expectancy Data

Our calculator uses the latest available life expectancy data from the World Health Organization and national statistics offices. Life expectancy varies significantly by country due to factors like healthcare quality, lifestyle, and socioeconomic conditions.

💡 Make Every Day Count

While these calculations provide interesting insights into your life timeline, remember that life expectancy is just an average. Focus on living a healthy, fulfilling life regardless of the numbers. Every day is a gift - that's why they call it the present!

Disclaimer: These calculations are for entertainment and educational purposes only. Life expectancy data is based on statistical averages and cannot predict individual lifespan. Many factors including genetics, lifestyle, healthcare access, and unforeseen circumstances affect actual lifespan.
